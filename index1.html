THE BIGGEST M FESTIVAL IN THE WORLD

It’s been a long 2 years, but SA’s biggest car festival is back and better than ever. Coming to the Kyalami Grand Prix Circuit in Johannesburg on the 25, 26 & 27 October, M FEST 2024 promises to be a tribute to the strongest letter in the world – M.
This is more than a festival - this is M FEST. Feel the adrenaline coursing through your veins with a full weekend of hot laps, exhilarating stunts, live entertainment and much more. Don’t miss your chance to experience M FEST 2024. Gates open at 14:00 and close at 21:00 on Friday and open at 09:00 and close at 21:00 on Saturday & Sunday. Mark your calendars.

What to expect at M fest 2024

As a M FEST attendee, you’ll get up close views of vehicles that aren’t officially retailing in South Africa yet
– not to mention access to limited edition lifestyle gear and merch on sale at M FEST 2024.
